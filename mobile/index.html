FAQ

Straight answers.

What's a DSCR loan, in English?
If the rent covers the mortgage payment, you qualify. No tax returns, no pay stubs. We look at the property, not your paycheck.
Can I close in an LLC?
Yes. Most of our investor loans fund directly to your LLC — clean title, clean books.
How much down?
As little as 15% for qualifying DSCR deals. More down → better rate. We'll show you the trade-off live.
How fast can you close?
Typical investor loans close in ~21 days. We've done 14 when the title work cooperates.
