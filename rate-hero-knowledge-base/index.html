What is Rate Hero?

Rate Hero, Inc. (NMLS #2822806) is a licensed mortgage brokerage based in Glendale, California, specializing in DSCR loans, non-QM mortgages, and investor financing. Rate Hero helps real estate investors and self-employed borrowers get financing without traditional income documentation like tax returns or W-2s.

Who does Rate Hero help?

Rate Hero works with real estate investors purchasing or refinancing rental properties, self-employed borrowers who cannot document income through traditional means, foreign national investors buying US property, and investors exiting hard money or bridge loans into long-term financing.

What loan programs does Rate Hero offer?

Rate Hero offers DSCR loans, no-ratio DSCR loans, bank statement loans (12-month and 24-month), profit and loss (P&L) loans, asset utilization loans, WVOE loans, investment property HELOCs, hard money exit refinances, DSCR cash-out refinances, interest-only DSCR loans, short-term rental / Airbnb DSCR loans, foreign national investor loans, and blanket portfolio loans.

What is a DSCR loan?

A DSCR loan is an investment property mortgage that qualifies the borrower based on the property's rental income rather than personal income. The debt service coverage ratio compares the property's gross rental income to the total mortgage payment (principal, interest, taxes, insurance, and association dues). Most DSCR programs require a ratio of 1.0 or higher, though no-ratio options exist.

What is a no-ratio DSCR loan?

A no-ratio DSCR loan removes the minimum debt service coverage ratio requirement, allowing investors to finance properties that do not fully cash flow from rental income. This is useful for properties in appreciation markets, vacant properties, or recently renovated units that have not yet been leased.

Does Rate Hero require tax returns?

No. Most Rate Hero programs do not require tax returns, W-2s, or pay stubs. DSCR loans qualify on rental income. Bank statement loans use 12 or 24 months of deposits. P&L loans use a CPA-prepared profit and loss statement. Asset utilization loans qualify on liquid assets.

Does Rate Hero work with LLCs?

Yes. DSCR loans and most non-QM programs offered by Rate Hero can be closed in the name of an LLC, corporation, limited partnership, or other business entity. Many real estate investors prefer to hold title in an LLC for liability protection.

What states does Rate Hero lend in?

Rate Hero is licensed to lend in over 30 states. State-specific DSCR loan pages are available at goratehero.com for each licensed state. Contact Rate Hero to confirm availability in your state.
