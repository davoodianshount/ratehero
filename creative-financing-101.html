02
Income Documentation
BANK
STATEMENT
LOANS
What It Is
Instead of relying on tax returns — which often show low income due to write-offs — bank statement loans use your actual bank deposits to determine qualifying income. 12 or 24 months of deposits replace the W-2.
How investors use it
🌱
New Investors
Buy your first property without waiting years for clean tax returns. Your deposits prove what you earn.
📈
Portfolio Builders
Qualify for additional rentals using business deposits — bypassing the write-off penalty entirely.
🏛
Large Investors
Streamline approvals by bypassing the complex tax write-offs that slow down conventional financing.
📋 Real Example
"You deposit $20K/month into your business account. Even if your tax return shows little profit after deductions, 24 months of statements prove your real cash flow — and qualify you for a much larger loan."
03
Investment Property · No Income Docs
📈 Long-Term Rental
DSCR
LONG-TERM
RENTAL
What It Is
The property qualifies itself. DSCR = Gross Rent ÷ PITIA. Qualifying rent is the higher of the Form 1007 market rent analysis or the current signed lease — as long as the variance is under 20%. No W-2s, no tax returns, no employment verification of any kind.
Key Features
🏠
No Income Documentation
The lease or 1007 market rent is the entire income case. No W-2s, no tax returns, no employment verification of any kind.
🏢
1–10 Unit Properties
SFR, 2–4 unit, and 5–10 unit multifamily all eligible. No portfolio cap — each property qualifies on its own cash flow independently.
🔑
Vacant Properties OK
No tenant required at application. The appraiser’s 1007 market rent qualifies at 100% — no vacancy factor applied on 1–4 unit properties.
🏯
LLC & Entity Borrowing
Close in an LLC or limited partnership. Keeps investment debt off personal credit and provides liability protection across the portfolio.
📋 Example
“BRRRR investor — rehab complete, no tenant yet. Appraiser’s 1007 projects $2,200/mo. PITIA $1,700. DSCR 1.29. Closed in LLC. Zero income documentation provided.”
04
Investment Property · No Income Docs
🏖 Short-Term Rental
DSCR
SHORT-TERM
RENTAL
What It Is
Same DSCR structure — but income is documented differently for Airbnb and VRBO properties. STR income can be established through AirDNA projections, 12-month platform history, or bank deposit records. When multiple sources are provided, the lowest figure is used to qualify.
Income Documentation Options
📊
AirDNA Rentalizer
For purchase transactions. 12-month forecast required. Market score must be 60 or higher. Minimum 3 comparable properties. 20% vacancy factor applied unless appraiser specifies occupancy rate.
📋
12-Month Platform History
Airbnb or VRBO statement covering the past 12 months, net of all management fees, identifying the subject property. Primary method for refinance transactions.
🏠
Bank Statement Deposits
12 months of bank statements showing STR deposits, paired with rental records for the property. Always verify HOA documents permit STR before application.
📋 Example
“Beachfront condo purchase, zero rental history. AirDNA market score 74, projects $4,800/mo gross. After 20% vacancy: $3,840 qualifying income. PITIA $2,800. DSCR 1.37 — approved on projections alone.”
DSCR Comparison · Section 05
LTR vs STR —
KNOW THE DIFFERENCE
Both qualify on rental income with zero personal income documentation. The distinction is how that income is proven and what thresholds apply.
📈 Long-Term Rental
STABLE.
SIMPLE.
SCALABLE.
Traditional 12-month leases. Consistent monthly income. The most straightforward DSCR qualification path available.
Income: Form 1007 or signed lease (higher, within 20%)
No vacancy factor on unleased 1–4 unit properties
No tenant required — 1007 market rent qualifies alone
5–10 unit multifamily eligible with investor experience
Up to 85% LTV on purchase · LLC borrowing available
No-ratio programs available when DSCR is below 0.75
Typical Use Case
BRRRR exit refi — rehab done, no tenant yet. 1007 projects $2,100/mo. PITIA $1,640. DSCR 1.28. Closes without a signed lease in hand.
🏖 Short-Term Rental
HIGHER
INCOME.
MORE DOCS.
Airbnb, VRBO, vacation rentals. Higher gross revenue potential — income documentation has more requirements and moving parts.
Income: AirDNA, platform history, or bank deposits — lowest of multiple sources
20% default vacancy unless appraiser specifies occupancy
AirDNA for purchases: market score 60+, 3 comparables, 12-month forecast
Platform history for refis: 12-month statement net of all fees
LTV may be slightly more conservative than LTR on some programs
Always verify HOA documents allow STR before application
Typical Use Case
Mountain cabin, no rental history. AirDNA market score 82, $5,200/mo gross. After 20% vacancy: $4,160. PITIA $2,950. DSCR 1.41 — approved on projections alone.
04
Wealth-Based Qualifying
ASSET
UTILIZATION
What It Is
Asset utilization lets you use savings, investment accounts, retirement funds, or life insurance as qualifying income — even without a paycheck. Your portfolio value is divided over a loan term to establish monthly income.
How investors use it
🌱
Retirees & High-Savers
Buy investment properties using accumulated wealth instead of active job income.
📈
Portfolio Builders
Use dormant assets to qualify for bigger loans without increasing taxable income.
🏛
Large Investors
Leverage investment portfolios while keeping active cash flow free for new acquisitions.
📋 Real Example
"You have $500K in stocks and retirement funds. Even without a W-2, lenders can count those assets as income — $500K ÷ 84 months = $5,952/month qualifying income."
05
Self-Employed
PROFIT
& LOSS
LOANS
What It Is
Instead of full tax returns, your CPA prepares a Profit & Loss statement — typically 12 months — to verify your business income. The net profit on the P&L becomes your qualifying income, bypassing the write-off problem.
How investors use it
🌱
New Investors
Get approved with CPA support even if your business is newly structured with limited history.
📈
Portfolio Builders
Use P&L when you reinvest heavily in your business and tax returns look weaker than reality.
🏛
Large Investors
Simplify approvals across multiple properties with streamlined documentation.
📋 Real Example
"Your tax return shows $22K taxable profit after write-offs, but your CPA-prepared P&L shows $150K net income — the P&L is what we use to qualify you."
06
Employment Verification
WVOE
LOANS
What It Is
WVOE stands for Written Verification of Employment. The lender verifies your job and income directly with your employer — no tax returns, no pay stubs required. Simple, fast, and effective for W-2 employees who want to skip the paperwork.
How investors use it
🌱
New Investors
Buy your first property quickly if you're employed — the lender calls your employer directly.
📈
Portfolio Builders
Keep it simple when stacking new loans alongside your salary income without tax return complexity.
🏛
Large Investors
Streamline approvals when combining W-2 income with investment financing in the same portfolio.
📋 Real Example
"You make $90K/year. Instead of digging through pay stubs and tax returns, Rate Hero confirms your salary directly with your employer — and you're approved."
07
Equity Access
HOME
EQUITY
LOANS
What It Is
Borrow against the equity in your current property without touching your low-rate first mortgage. A closed-end second or HELOC sits behind the existing first lien — giving you capital access while preserving your rate.
How investors use it
🌱
New Investors
Pull equity from your primary home to fund your first rental down payment without selling.
📈
Portfolio Builders
Use equity from existing rentals to fund renovations or down payments on additional properties.
🏛
Large Investors
Unlock millions in trapped equity across multiple properties without refinancing at current rates.
📋 Real Example
"Your home is worth $600K and you owe $300K. You can access up to $200K in equity to fund your next rental — all while keeping your existing low first mortgage rate completely intact."
What's Holding You Back
COMMON
MISTAKES
INVESTORS MAKE
📄
Believing they need "perfect" W-2 income to qualify
Bank statements, P&L, DSCR, and WVOE programs all exist specifically for borrowers without clean W-2s. Your tax return isn't the only way to prove you can repay a loan.
🏦
Assuming banks won't lend because of tax returns
Conventional banks rely on tax returns. Rate Hero and Non-QM programs were built specifically for borrowers whose write-offs make their income look artificially low on paper.
💤
Letting equity sit idle instead of using it to grow
Equity locked in a property earning 0% return is a missed opportunity. A home equity loan or HELOC puts that capital to work for the next acquisition without selling or refinancing.
🔍
Not exploring creative programs built for investors
Most investors get one conventional loan rejection and stop there. DSCR, bank statement, P&L, asset utilization — these programs exist and qualify borrowers every day. Know your options.
