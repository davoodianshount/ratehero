Short-Term Rental DSCR Loans in North Carolina — Asheville & OBX Guide

SD
Sean Davoodian, NMLS #1252107
NMLS #1252107 · April 8, 2026 · 5 min read

North Carolina has two of the most distinctive short-term rental markets on the East Coast: the Outer Banks beach market and Asheville's mountain arts scene. DSCR STR loans let investors finance these properties based on Airbnb revenue — no personal income documentation required.

Outer Banks DSCR STR

The Outer Banks is one of the highest-grossing vacation rental markets in the country per property. Large homes on Hatteras Island, Corolla, and Kill Devil Hills can generate $80,000–$150,000+ in annual gross rental revenue. At these revenue levels, DSCR ratios on even high-priced OBX properties are typically strong — often exceeding 1.4 on the STR income calculation.

For OBX properties, AirDNA is particularly valuable because actual rental history on well-established listings is often available. We use 12-month platform income history where available, and AirDNA projections for new acquisitions.

Asheville STR Financing

Asheville properties near downtown, the River Arts District, and Biltmore Village consistently rank among the top-performing STR assets in the Southeast. Year-round demand from food tourism, arts events, and mountain outdoor recreation creates diversified occupancy that reduces seasonal risk. Asheville STR DSCR loans require 25% down, 680+ credit, and AirDNA or platform income documentation.

Regulatory Note for NC STR

Both Asheville and Outer Banks municipalities have adopted STR regulations in recent years. Asheville requires an STR permit and limits the number of permits per property owner in some zones. OBX municipalities vary — some require permits, some don't. Rate Hero reviews local permissibility before closing on STR-designated properties in any NC market.

Why Use a DSCR Loan for NC STR

Conventional loans look at your personal income and often won't credit Airbnb revenue at all. DSCR loans qualify entirely on the property's income — which for a well-located OBX or Asheville STR can be 2–3x the equivalent long-term rental rate. This means more loan amount, higher LTV, and a qualification process that reflects how the property actually performs.
