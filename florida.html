How DSCR Works in Florida

The Property
Qualifies Itself

Florida's rental market is one of the strongest in the country. DSCR lenders look at one thing: does the rent cover the mortgage? If yes — you qualify. It doesn't matter what you make, how many write-offs you have, or what your tax return shows.

DSCR stands for Debt Service Coverage Ratio. It's calculated as:

Monthly Rent ÷ PITIA = DSCR
  • ✓DSCR 1.25+ — strongest programs, best rates
  • ✓DSCR 1.0–1.24 — qualifies on standard programs
  • ✓DSCR 0.75–0.99 — sub-1.0 programs available
  • ✓No-ratio options for unique situations
What You Need to Qualify

No W-2.
No Tax Returns.
No Problem.

Unlike conventional loans, DSCR loans in Florida are evaluated entirely on the property — not the borrower's income history.

  • ✓Credit score 620+ (680+ for best pricing)
  • ✓20–25% down payment on purchases
  • ✓Signed lease or Form 1007 market rent appraisal
  • ✓3–6 months reserves (varies by program)
  • ✓Property must be non-owner-occupied
  • ✓SFR, 2–4 unit, or condo (HOA STR rules apply)
  • ✓Available to LLC and entity borrowers

Common Questions

Florida DSCR
FAQ

Can I get a DSCR loan in Florida with no rental history on the property?+
Yes. For purchase transactions with no existing lease, the appraiser completes a Form 1007 market rent analysis. The projected market rent — not an actual lease — qualifies the loan. No tenant required at application.
Can I close a Florida DSCR loan in an LLC?+
Yes. LLC and entity borrowing is available on DSCR loans in Florida. This keeps investment debt off your personal credit profile and provides liability protection for your portfolio.
What's the minimum DSCR to qualify in Florida?+
Most standard DSCR programs require a minimum ratio of 1.0 — meaning rent covers the full mortgage payment. We also have sub-1.0 programs down to 0.75 for borrowers with strong credit and reserves. No-ratio programs exist for certain scenarios below 0.75.
Does Rate Hero do DSCR loans for Airbnb properties in Florida?+
Yes. Short-term rental DSCR loans are available for Florida Airbnb and VRBO properties. Income can be documented via AirDNA projections (for purchases), 12-month platform history, or bank deposit records. Orlando, Miami, and Tampa are among our most active STR markets.
How fast can Rate Hero close a DSCR loan in Florida?+
Our average close time on Florida DSCR loans is 21 days. Deals requiring a 1007 appraisal add a few days depending on appraiser availability in the market. We've closed as fast as 14 days on rate-and-term refinances with existing leases in place.
