How DSCR Works in Tennessee

The Property
Qualifies Itself

DSCR stands for Debt Service Coverage Ratio: Monthly Rent ÷ PITIA. If the property's rent covers the mortgage payment, it qualifies — regardless of your personal income, tax returns, or employment status.

Monthly Rent ÷ PITIA = DSCR
  • ✓DSCR 1.25+ — strongest programs, best rates
  • ✓DSCR 1.0–1.24 — qualifies on standard programs
  • ✓DSCR 0.75–0.99 — sub-1.0 programs available
  • ✓No-ratio options for the right borrower profile
Qualification Requirements

No W-2.
No Tax Returns.
No Problem.

DSCR loans evaluate the property — not the borrower's income history. What you need:

  • ✓Credit score 620+ (680+ for best pricing)
  • ✓20–25% down payment on purchases
  • ✓Signed lease or Form 1007 market rent appraisal
  • ✓3–6 months reserves (varies by program)
  • ✓Non-owner-occupied investment property
  • ✓SFR, 2–4 unit, or condo eligible
  • ✓LLC and entity borrowing available

Common Questions

Tennessee DSCR FAQ

Can I finance a Gatlinburg cabin with a DSCR loan?+
Yes — and Gatlinburg is one of our most active STR DSCR markets. Smoky Mountain cabin rentals often produce DSCR ratios of 1.5 or higher due to exceptional STR income relative to purchase prices. AirDNA projections, platform history, or bank deposits all qualify.
Does Rate Hero lend on Memphis investment properties?+
Yes. Memphis is one of the best cash-flow markets in the country. Properties in Memphis regularly produce DSCR ratios of 1.3–1.5 because rent is strong relative to purchase prices. We lend on SFR and 2–4 unit properties throughout the Memphis metro.
What's the minimum credit score for a Tennessee DSCR loan?+
Most Tennessee DSCR programs require a minimum 620 credit score. Borrowers with 680+ get access to the best rate tiers. We have programs available even if your score is between 620–660 — the program options are just slightly more limited.
Can I close a Tennessee DSCR loan in an LLC?+
Yes. LLC and entity borrowing is available on all DSCR programs in Tennessee. Closing in an LLC keeps investment debt off your personal credit profile and provides liability protection across your portfolio. We close a significant percentage of Tennessee deals in LLCs and limited partnerships.
How fast can Rate Hero close a DSCR loan in Tennessee?+
Our average close time on Tennessee DSCR loans is 21 days. Purchase transactions requiring an appraisal may add a few days depending on appraiser availability in your specific market. Rate-and-term refinances with existing leases in place can sometimes close in 14 days.
