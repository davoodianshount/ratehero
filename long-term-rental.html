Common Questions

Long-Term Rental FAQ

What is the minimum DSCR to qualify?+

Most programs require a DSCR of 1.0. We have select programs down to 0.75 for strong borrower profiles. The higher your DSCR, the better your rate and terms.

Can I use projected rent if the property is vacant?+

Yes. For purchases, we use market rent from a third-party appraisal even with no current tenant. For refinances, we use the existing lease or a market rent analysis.

Can I hold the loan in an LLC?+

Absolutely. We lend to LLCs, corporations, trusts, and other entities — a major advantage for investors who want to separate personal and investment assets.

Is there a limit on how many rental loans I can have?+

No limit. This is one of the biggest advantages of DSCR vs. conventional investment loans, which cap at 10 financed properties.
