DSCR Loans in Nashville, Tennessee — What Investors Need to Know

SD
Sean Davoodian, NMLS #1252107
NMLS #1252107 · April 8, 2026 · 6 min read

Nashville has transformed from a regional music city into a national real estate investment destination. Corporate relocations from California and the Northeast, a growing healthcare and tech sector, and strong population growth have pushed Nashville rental demand — and rental rates — significantly higher over the past five years. DSCR loans let investors capitalize on this demand without the W-2 income documentation barrier.

Nashville DSCR Investment Case

Tennessee has no state income tax on wages (the Hall Tax was fully eliminated in 2022), making it one of the most tax-friendly states for investors. For DSCR qualification, this matters less than the rental income — but it does mean the investor base in Nashville skews toward high-net-worth buyers who have complex income situations: business owners, investors, and out-of-state buyers with multi-state income. DSCR is the ideal program for all of them.

Nashville Submarkets for DSCR Investors

Antioch and Hermitage offer affordable SFR with strong tenant demand from healthcare and logistics workers. Murfreesboro (30 minutes south) is one of the fastest-growing suburbs in the country with MTSU driving rental demand. Germantown and East Nashville attract premium renters but at price points that compress DSCR ratios — better suited for appreciation-focused investors. Madison and Goodlettsville deliver the best cash-flow DSCR ratios in the metro.

Nashville STR — Bachelorette Party Capital

Nashville is consistently ranked as a top-5 US STR market for revenue per listing. The bachelorette party, corporate event, and country music tourism demand creates year-round occupancy with peak weekend rates that can be 3–4x long-term rental equivalent income. AirDNA income is accepted on Nashville STR DSCR loans — no rental history required for new acquisitions.
