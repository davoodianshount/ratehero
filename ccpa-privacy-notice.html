Your Rights Under the CCPA

As a California resident, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  1. Right to Notice — You will be notified of the personal data we collect, the purpose of collection, and the third parties we share it with.
  2. Right to Access — You may request a copy of the personal information we have collected from you. We will verify your identity before fulfilling the request.
  3. Right to Delete — You may request deletion of your personal information. Exceptions apply where retention is required to complete a service, comply with legal obligations, or for other lawful purposes.
  4. Right to Opt-Out of Sale — We do not sell your personal information. No opt-out is required, but you may contact us to confirm our practices.
  5. Right to Non-Discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of the rights described herein.
