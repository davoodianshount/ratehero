The Framework
WHAT BRRRR
ACTUALLY MEANS.
Each letter is a step. The magic is that if you execute the Refinance step correctly, you get most or all of your initial capital back — and repeat.
B
Buy — Distressed Property Below Market Value
You purchase a property that needs work — ideally 20–30% below its post-repair value. This can be financed with hard money, private money, or cash. The discount is where your profit gets built in.
R
Rehab — Renovate to Force Appreciation
Renovate the property to increase its market value (ARV — After Repair Value). The renovation is what justifies the higher appraised value at the refinance step. Good rehab = higher appraisal = more cash back.
R
Rent — Place a Tenant & Establish Income
Get a tenant in place before refinancing. The rent income is what the DSCR calculation uses to qualify. No W-2, no tax returns — the lease and rental income are your qualifiers.
R
Refinance — Pull Your Capital Back Out
This is where Rate Hero comes in. We refinance you out of the hard money loan into a permanent DSCR loan at 75–80% of the new appraised value. The cash-out pays back your renovation costs and often your down payment.
R
Repeat — Do It Again With Recycled Capital
Use the cash you pulled out of the refinance to fund the next deal's down payment and rehab. The same $80,000 can fuel multiple deals when the BRRRR math works.

The Refinance Step
HOW WE CLOSE
YOUR BRRRR REFI.
The refinance is the step that makes or breaks the BRRRR. Here's exactly how we execute it — and what can go wrong at each stage if you're not prepared.
📊
DSCR Qualification — Property Not You
No Income Documentation
The DSCR refi qualifies on the property's rental income divided by the new payment. We need the lease agreement (or market rent from the appraisal), not your W-2 or tax returns. Standard minimum: 0.75 DSCR for sub-1.0 programs, no-ratio (0.00 minimum) for qualified borrowers, 620 credit score.
🏠
Seasoning — How Long Must You Wait?
Timing Matters
Most DSCR lenders require 3–6 months of ownership before refinancing. Some have no seasoning requirement if using a DSCR program from day one. We'll tell you exactly what applies to your deal and timeline.
💰
Cash-Out LTV — How Much Can You Pull?
Typically 70–80% LTV
Cash-out DSCR refis typically go to 75% LTV. Some programs reach 80%. The higher the DSCR and credit score, the better the LTV available. This is the number that determines how much capital you recycle.
⚡
Speed — How Fast Can We Close?
21-Day Average
Hard money extensions cost 1–2% per month. Our average DSCR refi close is 21 days — which means you get out before the extension hits in most cases. We prioritize BRRRR exits for exactly this reason.
⚠️ The Most Common BRRRR Mistake
Waiting too long to contact a lender about the refinance. You should be in contact with Rate Hero before the rehab is finished — not after the tenant moves in and the hard money deadline is looming. Start the conversation 6–8 weeks before you need to close.
