Common Questions

VA FAQ

Do VA loans really require no down payment?+

Yes. Veterans with full entitlement can purchase with no down payment at any loan amount a lender approves. There's no VA-imposed loan limit with full entitlement. Borrowers with partial entitlement may need a down payment above their county's conforming limit.

What is the VA funding fee?+

The funding fee is a one-time charge that helps sustain the program. For a first-time purchase with no down payment, it's 2.15% of the loan amount; subsequent use is 3.30%. Putting 5% down lowers it to 1.50%, and 10% down lowers it to 1.25%. The IRRRL streamline refinance fee is just 0.50%. The fee can be financed into the loan.

Who is exempt from the funding fee?+

Veterans receiving VA disability compensation, those eligible to receive it, surviving spouses receiving Dependency and Indemnity Compensation, and active-duty Purple Heart recipients are exempt. Roughly one in three eligible veterans qualifies for a full exemption.

Can I use my VA benefit more than once?+

Yes. The VA loan benefit is reusable. Once you've paid off a prior VA loan or restored your entitlement, you can use it again. You can even have more than one VA loan at a time in some situations, depending on your remaining entitlement.
