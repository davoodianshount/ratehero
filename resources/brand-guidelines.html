01 · Who We Are
BRAND
STORY

Rate Hero exists because the mortgage industry was built to serve banks — not borrowers. We built a different kind of lending experience: one that works for the self-employed, the investor, the business owner, and anyone the big banks ignore.

We leverage our in-house expertise and deep lender relationships to find the right program for each individual — not the program that's easiest to approve. The lightning bolt isn't just a logo. It's a signal: fast, sharp, and built for people who don't have time for the ordinary.

"Your Income. Your Rules. Our Rates."

Mission

Make every borrower feel like the most important client in the room — then close their loan faster than they thought possible.

Vision

A world where self-employment, write-offs, and non-traditional income never prevent someone from building real estate wealth.

Values

Speed · Transparency · Expertise · No B.S. · Results over promises.

Clear Space Rule

Always maintain clear space equal to the height of the bolt icon on all sides of the logo. Never place other elements, text, or design within this exclusion zone. Minimum size: 24px tall in digital, 0.5in tall in print.

Design Do's & Don'ts
✓ Do
Use deep navy as the primary background. The brand lives on dark surfaces. Navy communicates authority, trust, and seriousness.
✕ Don't
Use white as the primary background on dark-mode materials. Light sections work as contrast breaks, not the primary canvas.
✓ Do
Lead with Bebas Neue for impact. Headlines should command attention. Pair with Plus Jakarta Sans body copy for readability.
✕ Don't
Use the amber/gold for anything other than the bolt and rare accent moments. Overuse destroys its signal value. Blue is the primary accent.
✓ Do
Use the subtle grid pattern as a background texture. The faint 56px blue grid adds depth without distraction on navy backgrounds.
✕ Don't
Use stock photography of generic families or handshakes. Rate Hero's audience is investors and business owners — not the conventional mortgage market.
