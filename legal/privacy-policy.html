Section 11

AI Chat Assistant ("Bolt")

Our Site offers an AI chat assistant, "Bolt," that answers questions about our loan programs and can connect you with a loan strategist. Bolt is built on large language models provided by Anthropic, PBC. When you use Bolt:

  • What we store. The full transcript of your chat (your messages and Bolt's responses), the page you were viewing, your browser type, and a cryptographic hash of your IP address. A randomly-generated session ID is saved to your browser's local storage so that if you continue the conversation later, we can pick up where you left off.
  • What we do not store. We do not store your raw IP address; only an irreversible SHA-256 hash used to identify automated abuse.
  • Lead submissions from chat. If you ask Bolt to have a strategist reach out, and you provide contact information (such as your name, phone, email, state, and loan situation), we submit that information to our lead management system in the same way we do for form submissions on the Site.
  • Third-party processing. Your chat messages are transmitted to Anthropic's API in order to generate Bolt's responses. Anthropic's use of that data is governed by its own privacy policy and commercial terms, which prohibit using API inputs or outputs to train its models.
  • Retention. Chat transcripts and associated metadata are retained indefinitely for quality assurance, lead follow-up, and operational analytics. You may request deletion of your transcripts at any time by emailing privacy@goratehero.com with the session ID shown at the top of your browser's local storage (or a reasonably specific description of the conversation).
  • No legal, financial, or regulatory advice. Bolt's responses are informational only and are not a commitment to lend, an approval, a rate lock, or an offer of credit. Final terms are always subject to underwriting review by a licensed loan officer.
  • California residents. You retain all rights under the CCPA/CPRA with respect to information collected via the chat assistant, including the right to know, the right to delete, and the right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information as defined under California law. See Section 5 above.

By using Bolt you consent to the collection, processing, and storage described above.
