How DSCR Works in Arkansas

The Property
Qualifies Itself

DSCR stands for Debt Service Coverage Ratio: Monthly Rent ÷ PITIA. If the property's rent covers the mortgage payment, it qualifies — regardless of your personal income, tax returns, or employment status.

Monthly Rent ÷ PITIA = DSCR
  • ✓DSCR 1.25+ — strongest programs, best rates
  • ✓DSCR 1.0–1.24 — qualifies on standard programs
  • ✓DSCR 0.75–0.99 — sub-1.0 programs available
  • ✓No-ratio options for the right borrower profile

Common Questions

Arkansas DSCR FAQ

Can I get a DSCR loan in Arkansas with no rental history?+
Yes. For vacant properties, the appraiser's Form 1007 market rent analysis qualifies the loan. No tenant or lease is required at application. This is standard across all our Arkansas DSCR programs.
Is Arkansas worth investing in for DSCR loans?+
Yes — particularly in Little Rock, Fayetteville, and the rapidly growing Northwest Arkansas corridor (Bentonville, Rogers, Springdale). These markets offer strong rent-to-price ratios with minimal institutional competition, allowing individual investors to find properties that easily exceed 1.3 DSCR.
What is the minimum loan amount for an Arkansas DSCR loan?+
Most of our DSCR programs have a minimum loan amount of $75,000–$100,000. Given Arkansas's low property prices, this is an important factor. We've identified lenders in our network who accept loan amounts as low as $75,000, which covers most Arkansas investment properties.
Can I close a Arkansas DSCR loan in an LLC?+
Yes. LLC and entity borrowing is available on all DSCR programs in Arkansas. Closing in an LLC keeps investment debt off your personal credit profile and provides liability protection across your portfolio. We close a significant percentage of Arkansas deals in LLCs and limited partnerships.
How fast can Rate Hero close a DSCR loan in Arkansas?+
Our average close time on Arkansas DSCR loans is 21 days. Purchase transactions requiring an appraisal may add a few days depending on appraiser availability in your specific market. Rate-and-term refinances with existing leases in place can sometimes close in 14 days.
