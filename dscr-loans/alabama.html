How DSCR Works in Alabama

The Property
Qualifies Itself

DSCR stands for Debt Service Coverage Ratio: Monthly Rent ÷ PITIA. If the property's rent covers the mortgage payment, it qualifies — regardless of your personal income, tax returns, or employment status.

Monthly Rent ÷ PITIA = DSCR
  • ✓DSCR 1.25+ — strongest programs, best rates
  • ✓DSCR 1.0–1.24 — qualifies on standard programs
  • ✓DSCR 0.75–0.99 — sub-1.0 programs available
  • ✓No-ratio options for the right borrower profile
Qualification Requirements

No W-2.
No Tax Returns.
No Problem.

DSCR loans evaluate the property — not the borrower's income history. What you need:

  • ✓Credit score 620+ (680+ for best pricing)
  • ✓20–25% down payment on purchases
  • ✓Signed lease or Form 1007 market rent appraisal
  • ✓3–6 months reserves (varies by program)
  • ✓Non-owner-occupied investment property
  • ✓SFR, 2–4 unit, or condo eligible
  • ✓LLC and entity borrowing available

Common Questions

Alabama DSCR FAQ

Is Alabama a good state for DSCR investors?+
Yes — Alabama consistently produces DSCR ratios of 1.3–1.5 on standard purchase prices because rents are strong relative to property costs. Birmingham, Huntsville, and Mobile are particularly strong markets where investors regularly qualify with DSCR well above the 1.25 threshold for best-rate programs.
Can I finance a Gulf Shores vacation rental with DSCR?+
Yes. Gulf Shores and Orange Beach are among our most active STR DSCR markets. Beach vacation rentals in Alabama's Gulf Coast produce strong income, especially May through September. AirDNA projections or 12-month platform history both qualify. Verify HOA or condo association STR rules before applying.
Does Rate Hero lend in Huntsville for investment properties?+
Yes. Huntsville is Alabama's fastest-growing city and has become one of our most active DSCR markets in the state. The city's tech and defense sector creates consistent high-income rental demand, producing strong DSCR ratios for investors.
Can I close a Alabama DSCR loan in an LLC?+
Yes. LLC and entity borrowing is available on all DSCR programs in Alabama. Closing in an LLC keeps investment debt off your personal credit profile and provides liability protection across your portfolio. We close a significant percentage of Alabama deals in LLCs and limited partnerships.
How fast can Rate Hero close a DSCR loan in Alabama?+
Our average close time on Alabama DSCR loans is 21 days. Purchase transactions requiring an appraisal may add a few days depending on appraiser availability in your specific market. Rate-and-term refinances with existing leases in place can sometimes close in 14 days.
