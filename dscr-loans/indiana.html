How DSCR Works in Indiana

The Property
Qualifies Itself

DSCR stands for Debt Service Coverage Ratio: Monthly Rent ÷ PITIA. If the property's rent covers the mortgage payment, it qualifies — regardless of your personal income, tax returns, or employment status.

Monthly Rent ÷ PITIA = DSCR
  • ✓DSCR 1.25+ — strongest programs, best rates
  • ✓DSCR 1.0–1.24 — qualifies on standard programs
  • ✓DSCR 0.75–0.99 — sub-1.0 programs available
  • ✓No-ratio options for the right borrower profile

Common Questions

Indiana DSCR FAQ

Why is Indianapolis so popular for DSCR investors?+
Indianapolis has one of the best rent-to-price ratios of any major US city. Properties that cost $200,000–$280,000 commonly rent for $1,700–$2,200/month, producing DSCR ratios well above 1.25 on 20% down. The city also has low property taxes and a strong employment base.
Does Rate Hero lend on Indiana properties under $150,000?+
Yes, with some caveats. Many DSCR lenders have minimum loan amounts of $75,000–$100,000. Properties below $150,000 can qualify if the loan amount meets the lender's minimum. We'll identify which of our top-tier lenders accepts the lower loan size for your specific property.
Can I finance multiple Indiana properties at once with DSCR?+
Yes. DSCR loans have no portfolio cap — each property qualifies independently on its own cash flow. Many of our Indiana investors close 3–5 properties per year by recycling equity through BRRRR refinances. There's no limit to how many DSCR loans you can hold with Rate Hero.
Can I close a Indiana DSCR loan in an LLC?+
Yes. LLC and entity borrowing is available on all DSCR programs in Indiana. Closing in an LLC keeps investment debt off your personal credit profile and provides liability protection across your portfolio. We close a significant percentage of Indiana deals in LLCs and limited partnerships.
How fast can Rate Hero close a DSCR loan in Indiana?+
Our average close time on Indiana DSCR loans is 21 days. Purchase transactions requiring an appraisal may add a few days depending on appraiser availability in your specific market. Rate-and-term refinances with existing leases in place can sometimes close in 14 days.
