DSCR Loans for Airbnb & STRs

AirDNA-accepted DSCR financing for Airbnb, VRBO, and short-term rentals. Qualify on STR income — purchase or refi.

Written by Sean Davoodian, NMLS #1252107 · Updated May 2026

DSCR loans for Airbnb and short-term rentals qualify the property using projected or actual short-term rental income instead of long-term lease rent. Lenders accept three documentation types: AirDNA market projections (used on purchases of vacant or new STRs), 12-month Airbnb or VRBO platform statements, and operating income summaries from the seller on stabilized properties. Maximum LTV is typically 75–80% on purchases and 70–75% on cash-out refinances.

STR income documentation methods

Three documentation paths exist. AirDNA Rentalizer projections are the most common method on purchases — the lender pulls a market projection for the subject address showing forecasted average daily rate, occupancy, and gross income; the underwriter applies a haircut (typically 75–80% of projected gross) before plugging the figure into the DSCR formula. Platform statements are used when the property has a 12-month booking history; the lender pulls actual gross income from Airbnb or VRBO statements and applies a vacancy adjustment. Operating income summaries (P&L statements) are used on transition acquisitions where the seller can document historical revenue. Some lenders accept all three, others restrict to AirDNA on purchases and platform statements on refinances.

Qualifying DSCR ratios for STR

STR DSCR is calculated the same way as long-term rental DSCR — gross income divided by PITIA — but with a vacancy and operating-expense adjustment baked into the income side. Most lenders use 75–80% of projected or actual gross as the qualifying figure, which accounts for the higher operating costs and seasonality of short-term rentals. A 1.0 DSCR is still the standard threshold, with sub-1.0 programs available. The mortgage payment side of the ratio includes any HOA dues and short-term rental permit fees if assessed by the property type. Some lenders also include cleaning fees in the operating expense haircut.

Local STR regulations and lender posture

Lenders increasingly screen for local STR regulation before underwriting. Properties in jurisdictions with outright STR bans (parts of New York City, much of Honolulu, certain Hawaii counties) are typically declined regardless of income. Restricted-zone properties — places like Austin, Nashville's downtown core, or coastal California cities with primary-residence-only STR rules — require documentation that the property qualifies for an existing STR permit. Properties in unrestricted markets (most of Florida, Tennessee outside Nashville core, Texas outside Austin core, the Smoky Mountains) underwrite normally. Always verify the property's STR eligibility before signing a contract — a failed regulatory check kills the income basis for the loan.

Best STR markets for DSCR financing

The most active STR DSCR markets in 2026 are: Florida (Orlando, Miami, Destin, the Panhandle), Tennessee (Gatlinburg, Pigeon Forge, the Smokies), Texas (the Hill Country, Galveston, the Gulf Coast), Arizona (Sedona, Flagstaff), Georgia (Blue Ridge, Helen), the Carolinas (Asheville, Outer Banks), and the Colorado mountain towns (Breckenridge, Steamboat). Strong-performing micro-markets share four traits: established tourism demand, permissive local STR rules, a price point under $750,000 that allows DSCR ratios to clear, and year-round (not just seasonal) booking demand. Properties in seasonal-only markets often need higher down payments to clear DSCR with the off-season vacancy adjustment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a DSCR loan for an Airbnb property?+
Yes. DSCR loans are available for short-term rentals using AirDNA projections, 12-month platform statements, or operating income summaries to document qualifying rental income.
Does AirDNA count as income documentation for a DSCR loan?+
Yes, on purchases and refinances of properties without an established platform history. Lenders typically use 75–80% of the AirDNA Rentalizer projection as the qualifying gross income figure.
What's the maximum LTV on a short-term rental DSCR loan?+
Purchase: 75–80% LTV depending on credit and DSCR ratio. Cash-out refinance: 70–75% LTV. Rate-and-term refinance: up to 80% LTV. Pricing tightens at higher LTV tiers.
Are STR DSCR loans available in cities with strict Airbnb regulations?+
Properties in outright STR bans are typically declined. Restricted-zone properties may qualify if they hold an existing STR permit. Verify local rules before applying — the regulatory check is part of underwriting.
What credit score is required for an STR DSCR loan?+
Same as long-term rental DSCR — 620 minimum on most programs, 660+ for the most competitive pricing tiers, 720+ for the best available rates. Some specialty STR programs require 680+ minimum.
